Audience


Target Audience Description

  • Demographics: This could be anyone looking to know the weather(e.g. school children, students, medical staff, teachers,artists, farmers, public servants)
  • Age: From 8 to 99
  • Motivation: Going to school, outdoors activities, Travel, leisure, business
  • Income: Not optional

Scenarios

  • A farmer is planning to go and harvest. What will the weather for the next three months?
  • Weakly weather forecast update is available on the site.
  • Accurate and correct weather information is provided to the customer.
  • Growing plants is risky, after so many rains, can I still plant?
  • Daily and annual weather forecast is avaialble on the site
  • Data are available about weather predictions of the year.
  • There are people coming from the entire province, will it rain?
  • Annual report is available through the weather website
  • Transport companies work together with the weather service bureau.
  • The annual marathon will be taking place soon, where can I train?
  • Available infographics as to where to go.
  • Registered cycling teams are provided with actual data and weather forecast for the year.
  • Are weather service data reliable, can I trust them?
  • Reliability - Daily information and update have been correct for the past 15 years.
  • Daily update of the weather information on the website.
