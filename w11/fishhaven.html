Fishing Tips From An Expert

By Elie Silvere Mambou

Fish Haven is the name of a town in Idaho, and it is also known for its amazing fishing spots tradition. We have asked one of the elderly brother in the city about his experience as a fisherman. He goes by the name of James Ollard, he is seventy years old, and he has been fishing for the past forty years. James has indicated that if you want to have a great fishing experience in Fish Haven, please visit the town during the summer season. This is the time to enjoy fishing as fishes are hungry following their annual migration. He also said that " he prefers to fish in the saratoga and hampster fishing pools, it is quiet and peaceful, above all rewarding."
